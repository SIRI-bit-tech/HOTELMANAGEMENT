About HotelPMS

We're on a mission to transform the hospitality industry with innovative technology solutions that empower hotels to deliver exceptional guest experiences.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of hospitality and technology experts, HotelPMS was born from the frustration of outdated, complex hotel management systems that hindered rather than helped hotel operations.

We recognized that hotels needed a modern, intuitive solution that could adapt to the rapidly changing hospitality landscape. Our team combined decades of hotel industry experience with cutting-edge technology to create a platform that truly understands the needs of modern hoteliers.

Today, HotelPMS serves over 500 hotels worldwide, from boutique properties to large hotel chains, helping them streamline operations, enhance guest satisfaction, and maximize revenue.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower hotels with innovative technology solutions that simplify operations, enhance guest experiences, and drive sustainable growth in the hospitality industry.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for hotel property management systems, enabling every hotel to deliver world-class hospitality through intelligent technology.
